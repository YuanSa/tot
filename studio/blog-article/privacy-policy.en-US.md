# Privacy Policy

## Overview

Our website only have a few limited functions, mainly contents display. We don't collect any personal information from you. When we have new functions, we will renew this policy in time.

If the policy's changes will affect our handling to your personal information, we will notify you in an obvious manner, explain the changes in an understandable way and ask you for your consents. We will not take any further action without your consent.

## Preference Records

To improve your experience, we will record your preference settings in you LocalStorage of the current web browser permanently.

> LocalStorage is a local storage space in your web browser. Our website's data in LocalStorage will not be sent in data transmission - all kept in your local device and only our website and yourself can access them. If you want to delete the data our website used, you may turn to the "Settings" page of your web browser and find the "Clear Cookies" (or any related) tools to clear Cookies and any other data, including LocalStorage mentioned here.

Our website will only record your choice, and will not collect any of your personal information. The detailed usage of the LocalStorage are listed below:

1. **Display language ("language")**: To record in which language do you prefer our website to be displayed in. We will download the corresponding language data to show. Our website now supports *Chinese* ("zh-CN") and *English* ("en-US"). However, some contents may have no corresponding language version (like some blog article). In this case, we will try to display contents in a similar language. If you haven't choose the language or chose *Follow System*, then this item will not be recorded and our website will be displayed in your web browser's language. If your web browser's language is not supported, our website will be displayed in a similar language or English. No extra data involved during this.
2. **Color theme ("theme")**: To record in which color style do you prefer our website to be displayed in. We will display our website styles according to your choice. Our website now supports *Light Mode* ("light") and *Dark Mode* ("dark"). If you haven't choose the color theme or chose *Follow System*, then this item will not be recorded and our website will be displayed in the color theme according to your web browser's instruction. For operation systems like *Mac OS*, *iOS* and *Android*, the "instruction" above means "system light/dark mode preference"; for operation systems like *Windows*, the "instruction" above doesn't apply, and our website will be displayed in *Light Mode*. No extra data involved during this.
3. **Article display width ("width")**: To record the maximum width the articled in our website will be displayed in you prefer. Our website will display articles according to your choice. Our website now supports *Narrow* ("narrow"), *Normal* ("normal"), *Wide* ("wide") and *Maximum* ("maximum"). If you haven't choose your preferred display width, then this item will not be recorded and our website will display articles in *Normal* width.
